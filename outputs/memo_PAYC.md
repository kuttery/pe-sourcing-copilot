# Sourcing Memo — Paycom Software, Inc. (PAYC)

# PAYCOM SOFTWARE, INC. (PAYC)
## PE SOURCING MEMO

---

### Company Overview
Paycom is a cloud-based HCM SaaS provider serving small to mid-sized U.S. businesses with an integrated platform spanning payroll, talent acquisition, talent management, and time & labor solutions. The company operates a recurring revenue model with $2.1B in annual revenue and serves as a comprehensive employment lifecycle management platform.

---

### Why It May Be Attractive for PE
Paycom scores 4.54/5 on our deterministic attractiveness framework, driven by strong unit economics and financial flexibility. The 3.3x EV/Revenue multiple is attractive for a SaaS company with 32% EBITDA margins and 14.1% FCF margins, and the 0.9x net debt/EBITDA ratio provides meaningful leverage capacity for acquisition financing.

---

### Key Strengths
- **Market Position & Scale:** $6.4B market cap with integrated, sticky HCM platform reduces customer churn risk and creates cross-sell opportunities
- **Elite Profitability:** 32% EBITDA margin is exceptional for SaaS and demonstrates pricing power and operational leverage
- **Financial Flexibility:** Low leverage (0.9x ND/EBITDA) and strong FCF generation ($294M annually at 14.1% margin) enable debt-financed acquisition
- **Recurring Revenue Model:** Cloud-based SaaS subscription structure provides revenue predictability and high gross margins

---

### Key Risks
- **Growth Deceleration:** 7.8% revenue growth is modest for a SaaS business and suggests market saturation or competitive pressures in SMB HCM
- **Limited Margin Expansion:** Already at 32% EBITDA, significant operational leverage improvements are constrained, limiting a core PE value driver
- **Market Dependency:** Concentrated exposure to U.S. SMB employment cycle creates sensitivity to economic downturns and hiring freezes
- **Competition & Consolidation:** Intense HCM market with larger competitors (Workday, ADP) and potential for disruptive entrants

---

### Possible Value-Creation Angles
- **Operating Leverage:** Pursue targeted cost optimization (G&A rationalization, sales efficiency) to drive 100-200bps EBITDA margin expansion despite growth constraints
- **M&A Bolt-Ons:** Acquire complementary HCM point solutions (benefits, wellness, HR analytics) to accelerate growth and cross-sell
- **International Expansion:** Extend platform to Canada or UK to diversify revenue beyond U.S. SMB exposure and reignite growth
- **Pricing & Mix:** Optimize subscription tier pricing and upsell higher-margin modules (advanced analytics, compliance) to existing customer base

---

### Next Diligence Questions
1. **Customer Economics:** What is customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), and net revenue retention (NRR)? Is the slowdown driven by market saturation or execution issues?
2. **Competitive Position:** How does Paycom's win rate and product roadmap compare to Workday, ADP, and mid-market alternatives? What is customer churn by cohort?
3. **M&A History & Capacity:** What bolt-on opportunities exist in the $50M–$500M range? Can management execute acquisition integration?
4. **Leverage & Covenants:** What is the current debt structure, and how much incremental debt capacity exists at target leverage (e.g., 4.5–5.0x)?
