# Sourcing Memo — Okta, Inc. (OKTA)

# PE SOURCING MEMO
**OKTA, INC. (OKTA)**

---

## COMPANY OVERVIEW
Okta is a leading cloud-based identity and access management (IAM) platform serving enterprises globally with a portfolio spanning single sign-on, adaptive multi-factor authentication, API access management, and emerging use cases like AI agent governance. The company has achieved $2.9B in annual revenue with 11.6% YoY growth and commands a $14.5B market capitalization in the competitive infrastructure software sector.

---

## WHY IT MAY BE ATTRACTIVE FOR PE
Okta presents a classically strong PE profile: substantial scale ($14.5B market cap), recurring SaaS revenue with healthy 28.6% free cash flow margins, and significant operational leverage opportunity given an 8.5% EBITDA margin. The company's fortress balance sheet (negative 8.4x net debt/EBITDA) provides substantial debt capacity to fund leveraged acquisition, while moderating growth and improving profitability reduce downside execution risk.

---

## KEY STRENGTHS
- **Cash generation**: 28.6% FCF margin demonstrates strong unit economics and working capital discipline
- **Balance sheet flexibility**: Negative net leverage enables 4-6x+ acquisition or dividend financing capacity
- **Market position**: Platform consolidation play with expanding TAM in identity governance and emerging AI security categories
- **Operational efficiency runway**: 8.5% EBITDA margin suggests 15%+ achievable through gross margin expansion and OpEx leverage

---

## KEY RISKS
- **Valuation entry**: 4.3x EV/Revenue remains above historical trough; limited margin of safety if cloud spending normalizes further
- **Competitive intensity**: Saturated IAM market with entrenched players (Ping, Azure AD, Okta competitors) may cap pricing power and churn risk under PE ownership
- **Growth deceleration**: 11.6% growth trajectory demands continuous new product adoption; execution missteps could trigger customer churn in sticky but discretionary software category

---

## POSSIBLE VALUE-CREATION ANGLES
- **Margin expansion**: Drive EBITDA margin from 8.5% to 14%+ through go-to-market optimization and R&D efficiency without compromising growth
- **M&A bolt-ons**: Consolidate point solutions in API security, identity governance, or privileged access to cross-sell and reduce CAC
- **Operational discipline**: Rationalize sales/marketing spend ratio and improve land-and-expand conversion metrics
- **Multiple arbitrage**: Improve margin profile and growth visibility to re-rate from 4.3x to 5-6x EV/Revenue at exit
